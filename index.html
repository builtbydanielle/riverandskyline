ABOUT RIVER & SKYLINE

Stories captured
in vivid detail

River & Skyline is a photography project dedicated to capturing authentic moments of life across Georgia and beyond with a focus on the beauty of real moments. Each image is created with intention, emotion, movement, and the magic found in preserving tiny pieces of time.

River & Skyline was started in the North Georgia area and is currently based near Macon, Georgia. Danielle started her freelance photography endeavors in 2010. Currently, her son, Eli, who has discovered a talent for capturing stunning images in his own style, is at his mom's side at weddings and events; they cover the entire state of Georgia, the southeast US region, and beyond.
